ABOUT

Austin Chatfield is a 16 year old music artist, singer, songwriter, guitarist, and pianist from Frisco Tx. Loves being on stage and sharing his love of music with the world. Started at a young age preforming all around Texas and has been singing everywhere from coffee shops to House of Blues, Six Flags, and Gas Monkey Live!  Born and raised in Texas, lives with his mom, dad, brother, and dog Toby. Has studied with School of Rock, Septien Entertainment group, and Studio 35.  Has an early background with acting and modeling with the Campbell Agency. Attends Texas Tech homeschool and pursues music full time. Diehard fan of the Dallas Cowboys. Ready to show the world what he’s got!
